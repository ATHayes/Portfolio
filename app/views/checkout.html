Checkout

Top Caters Ltd has hired you to create an order management system for their new 100 seat restaurant on Paul Street. The system should handle day to day purchases in the restaurant and save this data.

Menu items are presented in a grid, ordered by category. The cashier enters the order and proceeds to the checkout.

The manager can add new items and edit the prices of these items. They simply have to press one of the buttons shown and a dialog will appear allowing them to enter the new item and it's price. We used the design metaphor of buttons to allow the manager to quickly and intuitively add items to the menu. All new specials are saved to the database, so they will appear when the next cashier signs in.

Data related to items, orders and employees is persisted in an embedded Apache Derby Database.
